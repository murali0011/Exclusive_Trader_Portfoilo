About Exclusive Trader

From luxury travel to supply chain excellence - our journey of transformation

Our Journey

Exclusive Traders (ET) was founded in 2010, initially operating as Exclusive Travel, a key provider of premium travel and hospitality services within the competitive London luxury sector.

For over a decade, this dedicated focus granted us deep, invaluable operational knowledge of the UK market. We gained first-hand insight into the intense operational demands and the high-level quality required to maintain a seamless, five-star guest experience.

This foundational experience revealed a critical, chronic issue: a supply chain gap for consistently reliable, high-quality essential luxury and operational items, coupled with unnecessary intermediary costs.

Our Transformation

We responded by undergoing a strategic transformation, pivoting ET to become a specialized B2B sourcing and procurement partner dedicated to solving this very problem. Our new mission is to deliver Supply Chain Certainty.

ET leverages its robust global network, built on years of international trade, to secure Direct-to-Manufacturer Access. This crucial model removes the layers of traditional distribution, directly benefiting our clients with superior quality control at the source and guaranteed competitive pricing.

Our Portfolio

Our curated portfolio is segmented to directly support premium hospitality operations, eliminating the need to juggle multiple, unreliable suppliers. We focus on three critical areas:

  • Premium Guest FMCG: Including custom-branded amenities, sustainable consumables, and luxury linens that elevate the visitor experience.
  • Operational FMCG: High-volume, high-efficiency commercial cleaning solutions and kitchen essentials for seamless back-of-house operations.
  • Strategic Raw Materials: Specialized products for facility maintenance, refurbishment, and enhancing long-term asset value.

Our Commitment

We are fully committed to UK/EU regulatory compliance and transparent, ethical sourcing across all partnerships. We act as a stable, reliable extension of your procurement team.

Our guiding principle remains the foundation of our existence: "Where there is need is the answer one can get from us."
