About Exclusive Trader

From luxury travel to supply chain excellence - our journey of transformation

Exclusive Traders (ET) was founded in 2010, initially operating as Exclusive Travel, a key provider of premium travel and hospitality services within the competitive London luxury sector.

For over a decade, this dedicated focus granted us deep, invaluable operational knowledge of the UK market. We gained first-hand insight into the intense operational demands and the high-level quality required to maintain a seamless, five-star guest experience.

This foundational experience revealed a critical, chronic issue: a supply chain gap for consistently reliable, high-quality essential luxury and operational items, coupled with unnecessary intermediary costs.

We responded by undergoing a strategic transformation, pivoting ET to become a specialized B2B sourcing and procurement partner dedicated to solving this very problem. Our new mission is to deliver Supply Chain Certainty.
